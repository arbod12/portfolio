Why I built all this

I'm studying accounting at Fairleigh Dickinson and interning as an auditor, but I kept noticing the same thing: a lot of the work people do by hand is work a computer should be doing. So I started building the tools myself.

Everything here is something I designed, coded, and deployed on my own. No templates, no website builders. I wanted to show that I can take a messy real-world problem, think it through, and turn it into something that actually works.

The goal is simple. As more of the world runs on AI and automation, I want to be one of the people who builds it, not one of the people it replaces.

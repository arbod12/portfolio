My motivation for this website

I'm currently studying accounting at Fairleigh Dickinson University, and couldn't help but notice the same pattern in our society: a lot of the work people do by hand is work a computer could be doing. So I started building the tools myself.

Everything on this website is something I designed, coded, and deployed on my own. My intention was to show that I can take a real-world problem and solve it using a specific tool.

As more of the world runs on AI and automation, I want to be one of the people who helps build it.

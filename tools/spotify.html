Spotify Player

Connect your Spotify account and play your own playlists right here. In-browser playback needs Spotify Premium. Your login is handled securely in your browser (PKCE) — no password ever touches this site.

One-time setup

To talk to Spotify, this player needs a free Client ID from a Spotify developer app. You make one in about two minutes, paste it below, and never touch it again (it is stored only in your browser).

  1. Go to developer.spotify.com/dashboard and log in with your Spotify account.
  2. Click Create app. Name it anything (e.g. “My Site Player”).
  3. In Redirect URIs, paste this exact address and click Add:
  4. For Which API/SDKs are you planning to use, tick Web API and Web Playback SDK. Save.
  5. Open the app → Settings → copy the Client ID and paste it here:

New Spotify apps start in Development mode, which only allows accounts you list. In the app go to Settings → User Management and add your own Spotify name + email so you can log in.
